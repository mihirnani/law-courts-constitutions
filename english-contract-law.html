The Common Law
of Bargain

English contract law was not made by a single theory of promise. It grew through forms of action, commercial practice, equity, legislation and repeated judicial reconstruction.

Scope and date. This page concerns the law of England and Wales and was reviewed against primary legislation and appellate judgments in June 2026. It combines historical explanation with present doctrinal orientation. Case summaries are not legal advice; use the linked judgments, reports and legislation for authoritative text.
